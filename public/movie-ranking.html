My top 3 movies of all-time

The Godfather(1972)

It transformed the "mob movie" into a tragic Greek epic about family and power.
It is a masterclass storytelling.

The Shawshank Redemption(1994)

It deeply emotional story about hope, patience, and friendship.

Schindler's List

It is a harrowing but essential look at the best and worst of humanity.
